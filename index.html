Packages We Offer

We offer different kinds of packages, depending on your request(CUSTOMERS).We offer,Appetizers / Starters,Soup Course,Main Course,Side Dishes,Dessert,Drinks / Beverages

Appetizers / Starters

Small bites served before the main meal to stimulate appetite. Examples: Spring rolls Puff-puff and samosa Chicken wings Mini meat pies Canapés e.t.c

Soup Course

Light soup served before the main course. Examples: Cream of chicken Egusi or Ogbono soup (served with small swallows in Nigerian style) Pepper soup (goat, fish, or chicken) e.t.c

Main Course

The heaviest and most important meal of the service. Examples: Jollof rice, Fried rice, or Coconut rice Pounded yam, Amala, Semovita with soup Spaghetti or Pasta dishes e.t.c

Side Dishes

Complement the main course. Examples: Plantain (fried or boiled) Salad (coleslaw, vegetable) Moi-moi or Akara Yam porridge e.t.c

Desserts

Sweet dishes served after the main course. Examples: Ice cream Cakes or pastries Fruit salads Chin chin e.t.c

Drinks/Beverages

Drinks / Beverages Served throughout or after meals. Examples: Cocktails and mocktails Zobo, Chapman Soft drinks or juices Water (still or sparkling) e.t.c
